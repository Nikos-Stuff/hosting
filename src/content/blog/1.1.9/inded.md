## Free server flow is complete
We’re optimizing our resources! To keep things running smoothly, free servers will now be suspended after 7 days of inactivity. ( This was already implemented - but we're tweaking it a bit )

If you don’t log in within 3 days of suspension, your server will be deleted. Don't worry—we’ll send a backup link to your email, which will be available for a few days. Simply log in once a week to keep your server online! This action will ensure that we have enough resources to provide a stable & better experience for everyone. Thanks for understanding!

That's all for now! See you soon!
